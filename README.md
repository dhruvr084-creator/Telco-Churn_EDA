# 📊 Telco Customer Churn Analysis

## 🔍 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing customer churn behavior in a telecommunications company. The goal is to identify key factors that lead customers to leave (churn) and uncover actionable insights that can help improve customer retention.

Churn is a critical business problem—acquiring new customers is often more expensive than retaining existing ones. This analysis aims to support data-driven decision-making by highlighting patterns and trends in customer behavior.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Understand the main drivers of customer churn
* Perform exploratory data analysis (EDA) to uncover patterns
* Identify high-risk customer segments
* Provide business insights and recommendations to reduce churn

---

## 📁 Dataset

The dataset contains customer information such as:

* Demographics (gender, senior citizen status, etc.)
* Account details (tenure, contract type, payment method)
* Services subscribed (internet, phone, streaming, etc.)
* Billing information (monthly charges, total charges)
* Churn status (Yes/No)

   Dataset Download source:
        https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/blastchar/telco-customer-churn

## 📊 Key Steps

1. **Data Cleaning**

   * Handling missing values
   * Correcting data types
   * Removing inconsistencies

2. **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)**

   * Distribution analysis
   * Correlation checks
   * Visualization of churn patterns

3. **Feature Analysis**

   * Impact of contract type on churn
   * Effect of monthly charges and tenure
   * Service usage behavior

4. **Insights & Findings**

   * Customers with month-to-month contracts are more likely to churn
   * Higher monthly charges correlate with increased churn risk
   * Long-term customers show lower churn probability

---

## 📈 Results & Insights

* Short-term contracts and high billing are major churn drivers
* Customers without add-on services are more likely to leave
* Retention strategies should target early-stage customers

---

## 💡 Recommendations

* Encourage long-term contracts through incentives
* Offer bundled services to increase engagement
* Provide targeted offers for high-risk customers
